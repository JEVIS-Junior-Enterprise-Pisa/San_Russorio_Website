Booking Information

Everything you need to know before booking your stay at San Russorio B&B

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM - 8:00 PM (Please contact us for late check-in)
  • Check-out time: By 11:00 AM

Policies

  • Cancellation policy: Free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival. Cancellations made less than 7 days before arrival are subject to a charge of the first night's stay.
  • Children: Children of all ages are welcome.
  • Pets: Sorry, pets are not allowed.
  • Smoking: No smoking inside the rooms or common areas. Designated smoking areas are available in the garden.

Payment Information

  • Accepted payment methods: Cash, Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), Bank Transfer
  • Deposit: A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking.
