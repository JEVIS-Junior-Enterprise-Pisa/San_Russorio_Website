Our Story

Nestled in the rolling hills of the Italian countryside, San Russorio B&B offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Our family-owned bed and breakfast combines traditional Italian hospitality with modern comforts.

Built in the early 19th century, our villa has been lovingly restored to preserve its historic charm while providing all the amenities today's travelers expect. Each room is uniquely decorated with local antiques and artwork, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.
