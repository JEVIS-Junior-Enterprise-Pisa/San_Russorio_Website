Find Us

San Russorio B&B is located in the heart of Tuscany, just a short drive from Montepulciano and other charming towns in the region.

Directions

From Florence (1.5 hours by car)

  1. Take the A1 highway south toward Rome
  2. Exit at Valdichiana
  3. Follow signs to Montepulciano
  4. In Montepulciano, follow signs to San Russorio

From Rome (2 hours by car)

  1. Take the A1 highway north toward Florence
  2. Exit at Chiusi-Chianciano Terme
  3. Follow signs to Montepulciano
  4. In Montepulciano, follow signs to San Russorio

By Train

The nearest train stations are Chiusi-Chianciano Terme (25 km) and Montepulciano Station (15 km). From either station, you can take a taxi or bus to reach us.

We can arrange a pickup service from the train station for an additional fee. Please contact us in advance to arrange this service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about staying at San Russorio B&B

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM. If you plan to arrive outside these hours, please contact us in advance. Check-out is by 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, a delicious homemade breakfast featuring local and seasonal ingredients is included in all room rates. Breakfast is served from 7:30 AM to 10:00 AM in our dining room or, weather permitting, in the garden.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers from Florence or Rome airports for an additional fee. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to arrange this service.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free private parking on-site for all our guests.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and lactose-free options. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when making your reservation.
